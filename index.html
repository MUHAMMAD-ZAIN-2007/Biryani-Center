Biryani ka naam sunte hi bhookh aur khushi dono ek saath jag uthe. Yeh ek aisi dish hai jismein masalon ki garmi, chawal ki narmi aur gosht ki lazzat milkar ek shandar tajurba paida karti hai. Har plate ke saath ek kahani judi hoti hai - kabhi dosto ki mehfil ka hissa, kabhi ghar ki yaadon ka zariya, aur kabhi shaadiyon ka shor-sharaba. Biryani sirf khanay ka naam nahi, balki mohabbat aur tehzeeb ka nishan hai jo logon ko ek hi dastarkhwan par jor deti hai.
